The Mystery of Mr. E

The Unknown Resident of the American RapCity

Mr. Melvin Rodrigo Edwards (or Mr. E for short) is a resident of the fictional city known as the American RapCity. He only appears to come out of his home every day at the same time and when he does, it's to get some food from a diner and perform a rap song for the other customers. Other than that and his full name, almost nothing is known about him and if you head to his mobile home and knock on the door, there will be no answer.

What We Know

  • Life: Melvin was born on March 28, 2006 and was raised somewhere in Arizona before moving to the American RapCity at age 19.
  • Education: Although he was always homeschooled, Melvin did start attending online schools at age 10. He attended the following schools at different ages:
    • Arizona Connections Academy in elementary school
    • Primavera Online Middle School in junior high
    • ASU Preparatory Academy Digital in high school
  • Hobbies: He may not have a lot of known hobbies, but it is known that he has them and they are listed as the following:
    1. Performing raps live at the Hunger Town local diner
    2. Drawing comic strips for The American Snitch newspaper
    3. Designing video games
